## Overview

A research project that integrates **human mobility data** into a domain knowledge graph of flood hazards, critical infrastructure, and vulnerable populations, and uses it to **ground large language models (LLMs)** for generating actionable, neighborhood-level flood warning advisories.

## Objectives

- Construct a spatio-temporal knowledge graph linking mobility patterns, flood inundation forecasts, and social-vulnerability indicators.
- Use retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to let an LLM produce location-aware warning messages tailored to different communities.
- Evaluate message quality, timeliness, and downstream behavioral impact with stakeholders.
