Delivered a guest lecture on research thinking and GeoAI in a graduate Research/Proposal course at The University of Alabama — my first time teaching in a formal course setting.

Covered a "research thinking" toolkit: how to frame a research question, clarify contribution, and think about evidence and limits. The discussion with students offered new perspectives from diverse research areas.
